Culture Bump Trainings


Culture Bump has 40 years of experience training individual, groups, and corporate employees in the skills they need to deepen their cross cultural communication competency.Over that period, Culture Bump trainings have been refined into process proven programs that result in an increased ability to manage "culture bumps" or cultural differences and sustain connection with people from differing backgrounds. Find out more details by choosing the category of training that best suits your needs.


Individual Trainings

Culture Bump is dedicated to helping individuals understand their culture and how it effects them during their interactions with people from different cultural backgrounds.

Group Trainings

Culture Bump has a long history of training groups in developing their cross culture communications skills.


Corporate Trainings

Culture Bump offers programs designed to fit the cross cultural communication training needs of businesses.
